# FEE Argentina Programmes

FEE Argentina administers **five international programmes** from FEE's global headquarters in Copenhagen, Denmark.

---

## 1. Eco-Schools

An educational programme for schools to embed sustainability in the curriculum and in institutional management.

- **Website:** [www.ecoescuelas.org.ar](http://www.ecoescuelas.org.ar)

---

## 2. Blue Flag

A certification programme for beaches, marinas and boating tourism operators that meet strict environmental quality, safety and education criteria.

- **Website:** [www.banderaazul.org.ar](http://www.banderaazul.org.ar)

---

## 3. Green Key

A certification programme for hospitality, tourism and leisure establishments that demonstrate a commitment to environmental sustainability.

- **Website:** [www.greenkey.org.ar](http://www.greenkey.org.ar)
- **International:** [www.greenkey.global](http://www.greenkey.global)

---

## 4. Young Reporters for the Environment (YRE)

A programme that promotes environmental journalism among young people, training them to investigate and communicate local and global environmental issues.

- **Website:** [www.jovenesreporteros.org.ar](http://www.jovenesreporteros.org.ar)

---

## 5. Learning about Forests (LEAF)

A learning programme about ecosystems and forests aimed at school communities.

- **Website:** [www.leaf.global](http://www.leaf.global)
