# History of FEE Argentina

## Founding and global reach

The Foundation for Environmental Education (FEE) was established in **1981** and has become one of the largest environmental education organizations in the world.

It currently has more than **100 member organizations** across **81 countries**.

## Experience and strategy

With four decades of experience in education for sustainable development, the organization launched its strategic plan known as **"GAIA 20:30."**

This approach prioritizes:
- Climate action
- Fighting climate change
- Reducing biodiversity loss
- Combating pollution

## Educational programmes

The foundation runs initiatives that train young people through solutions-based approaches:

- **Eco-Schools**
- **Learning about Forests (LEAF)**
- **Young Reporters for the Environment (YRE)**

## International recognition programmes

**Green Key** and **Blue Flag** are initiatives that promote sustainable business practices and the protection of natural resources.

## Institutional vision

> "A sustainable world in which education creates positive change for everyone."

## Headquarters

The international head office is located in **Copenhagen, Denmark**.
The Argentine chapter (FEE Argentina) operates from **Buenos Aires, Argentina**.
