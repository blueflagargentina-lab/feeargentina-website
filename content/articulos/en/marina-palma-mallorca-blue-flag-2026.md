Palma Marina, one of the busiest marinas in the western Mediterranean, confirmed the renewal of its Blue Flag certification for marinas for 2026. The award, granted separately from the beach certification, assesses environmental management of marinas, nautical safety and the services available to crews.

## New electric infrastructure

Among this year's additions, port authorities installed six new charging stations for electric and hybrid boats, in line with growing demand for low-impact navigation in the Balearic region. The wastewater and boat-maintenance residue collection system was also expanded, one of the most demanding aspects of the certification programme.

## Standards for marinas

The Blue Flag scheme for marinas requires, among other criteria, the separation of hazardous waste, accessible public restrooms, and environmental education programmes aimed at sailors. Annual renewal depends on audits confirming sustained compliance with these standards throughout the season.
