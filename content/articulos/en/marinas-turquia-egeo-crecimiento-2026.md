Turkey established itself in 2026 as one of the fastest-growing countries in Blue Flag marina certifications, doubling the number of awarded sites along the Aegean coast compared to five years earlier. The figure comes from records kept by the Foundation for Environmental Education (FEE), which administers the programme globally.

## Investment in nautical infrastructure

Turkish authorities attribute the growth to a marina modernization plan that included wastewater treatment systems, improved safety signage, and environmental training programmes for port staff. These conditions are reviewed annually by independent auditors before each certification is renewed.

## A growing boating destination

The expansion of Turkey's boating sector is also reflected in a rise in available berths for rental and charter vessels, a segment that saw sustained demand in 2026 from European tourists who prioritize certified marinas when planning trips across the Aegean and eastern Mediterranean.
